## Section 5: Misrepresentation of Statutory Liability

### Buzzworks's Legal Position:

Statement by Buzzworks:
> "As per Section 21 of The Contract Labour (Regulation and Abolition) Act, the Principal Employer shall be responsible..."

### Correct Legal Position:
1. Primary Liability: Under the Maternity Benefit Act, 1961, the immediate employer (Buzzworks) bears primary liability for maternity benefits
2. Section 21 of CLRA: Provides for recovery mechanisms from principal employer, not absolution of contractor's primary liability
3. Judicial Precedent: Established case law confirms contractors cannot evade maternity benefit obligations by citing client arrangements

### Evidence of Direct Employment Relationship:
- Offer letter issued by Buzzworks
- Salary disbursements from Buzzworks
- Employment relationship established with Buzzworks
- Provident Fund and ESIC contributions made by Buzzworks
